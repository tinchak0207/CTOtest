About CPTTM

The Macau Productivity and Technology Transfer Center (CPTTM) was founded in 1996 as a non-profit organization jointly established by the Macau SAR Government and the private sector.

Our mission is to support enterprises to utilize effectively new conceptual thinking, information and resources in order to increase the value-added of its products or services, with the aim to promote higher productivity and stronger competitiveness.
